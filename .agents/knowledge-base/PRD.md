## AI Startup Project
**Philosophy: Business First, AI Engine**

* **Goal:** Build an AI-driven startup solving a real market pain point.
* **Focus:** AI efficiency can transform traditional models; it's not just about selling AI.
* **Format:** Welcomes both Human-in-the-loop and Autonomous Agent solutions.

---

## Deliverables

### 1. Business Plan
* Market, value proposition, and GTM strategy.
* **GenAI Log:** Appendix detailing use of AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) in ideation/development.

### 2. Full-Stack Prototype
* Working UI (Frontend) and AI Backend.
* Use platforms like Firebase, Streamlit, or Project IDX (no command-line only).
* Demonstrate step-by-step execution from prompting to integration.

---

## Financials & Unit Economics

### Cost Structure ("Silicon" Cost)
* **Inference:** API call token costs (e.g., Gemini 1.5 Pro, GPT-4o) per user/transaction.
* **Hosting:** Cloud GPU (AWS/GCP) costs for local/open-source models (e.g., Llama 3).
* **Data:** Vector DB storage for RAG.

### Revenue Model
* Show clear path to profitability based on AI economics.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

1. **Commercial Feasibility:** Real problem solved? Sustainable financial model/ROI?
2. **Technical Execution:** Does the full-stack prototype work?
3. **Defensibility & Safety:** What is your "Moat"? How are hallucinations and privacy handled?
4. **Presentation:** Pitch delivery.

---

## Dual Judging Panel

* **Carbon Judges (Human):** PhD-level staff/faculty evaluating business logic, market fit, and presentation.
* **Silicon Judges (AI):** Specialized LLM evaluating business plan and architecture. Pressure-tests logic, edge cases, and cost structure.
* **Academic Integrity:** Hidden GenAI usage or unacknowledged resources results in penalties (warning, deduction, or zero).
